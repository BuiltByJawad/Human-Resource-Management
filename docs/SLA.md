# Service Level Agreement (SLA)

**Service:** HRM Platform  
**Uptime Target:** 99.9% monthly

---

## Availability
- **Monthly uptime commitment:** 99.9%
- **Measurement:** External uptime monitoring
- **Exclusions:** Scheduled maintenance, force majeure

## Support Response Times
| Severity | Response Time | Update Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- |
| P1 | 1 hour | Every 30 minutes |
| P2 | 4 hours | Every 2 hours |
| P3 | 1 business day | Daily |
| P4 | 2 business days | Weekly |

## Maintenance Windows
- Planned maintenance communicated at least 48 hours in advance
- Typically scheduled during off-peak hours

## Status Page Setup
- Maintain a public status page (e.g., Statuspage, Better Stack)
- Publish incident updates during outages and degradations
- Include current uptime metrics and incident history

## Credits
Service credits (if any) are defined in the commercial contract.
